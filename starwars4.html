Overview

Recon Company is a specialist element focused on precise marksmanship, stealthy insertion, and high-value target elimination. Operating ahead of the main force, recon squads gather critical intelligence, neutralize key threats, and disrupt enemy command and control to shape the battlefield for 212th operations.

Mission Set

  • Primary: Long-range reconnaissance and precision target elimination.
  • Secondary: Forward observation for artillery and air support.
  • Tertiary: Covert sabotage and disruption of enemy logistics.

Doctrine

Recon squads operate in small, highly trained teams with an emphasis on concealment, fieldcraft, and patient engagement. Fire-and-move tactics are minimized — a single, well-placed shot can decide the outcome. Coordination with command elements is vital to ensure intel is exploited and targets identified are engaged at optimal times.

Training

Training emphasizes marksmanship at varied ranges, camouflage & concealment, observation techniques, and exfiltration under duress. Advanced candidates train as spotters, forward observers, and stealth infiltration specialists capable of independent operations behind enemy lines.

IN VISIBILITY: The Recon Company moves where others cannot — unseen, unheard, decisive.

STRIKE: One shot, one kill. Precision is the creed; patience is the weapon.

REVEAL: Their intel carves the battlefield — information is as lethal as any cannon.

Equipment

  • Long-range sniper rifles with enhanced optics.
  • Camouflage cloaks and suppressors for silent engagements.
  • Surveillance drones and long-duration observation kits.
  • Silenced sidearms and close-quarters breaching tools.

Operational Notes

  • Operators must be self-sufficient and able to operate for extended periods without resupply.
  • Strict communication protocols to avoid compromising positions.
  • Prioritize stealth and intel exfiltration over direct engagement unless mission-critical.
