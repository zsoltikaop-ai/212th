212th Battalion — Ghost Company

Armored Infantry • Juggernauts • AT‑TE Operations
Designation: Infantry Company • Status: Open

Ghost Company is one of the two standard infantry companies of the 212th Battalion. Tasked with armored warfare and direct assaults, they represent the armored might of the division. Their operations rely heavily on heavy vehicles such as AT‑TEs and Juggernauts, allowing them to spearhead assaults, defend fortified positions, and sustain prolonged engagements under heavy fire.

Command Structure

Commanding Officer: CO‑8432 “Lemon”
Field commander and armored warfare specialist.
Executive Officer: CO‑9922 “Hades”
Supports logistics, armor deployment, and operational coordination.

Mission Set

Primary: Armored spearhead operations using AT‑TEs and Juggernauts to crush enemy positions and secure objectives.

Secondary: Heavy infantry support for mechanized and airborne elements of the 212th.

Tertiary: Defensive fortifications and convoy escort under heavy resistance.

Doctrine & Combat Role

Ghost Company employs a doctrine of overwhelming firepower and resilience. Their tactical strength lies in armor coordination — infantry supported by tanks and walkers for combined-arms efficiency. Infantry squads operate close to armored units, protecting vulnerable vehicle flanks and clearing obstacles to maintain forward momentum during sieges or urban combat.

Training & Logistics

Members of Ghost Company undergo specialized vehicle training for maintenance and gunnery on Republic heavy armor. Their cross-training ensures flexibility, allowing troopers to rotate between infantry and vehicle crews. Logistics units emphasize fuel management, repair operations, and tactical redeployment under combat conditions.

Vehicles & Equipment

  • AT‑TE: Primary assault walker for mobile fire support and siege warfare.
  • Juggernaut (HAVw A6): Heavy transport and front-line breakthrough vehicle.
  • AT‑RT & BARC Speeders: Reconnaissance and fast-response support.
  • Infantry Gear: Reinforced armor with integrated HUDs and comm systems designed for vehicle coordination.

Company Legacy

Ghost Company is renowned for their stoic discipline and mechanical precision. They have fought in some of the most brutal sieges of the Clone Wars, their walkers and Juggernauts forming the steel backbone of Republic offensives. Their call sign — “Ghosts” — stems from their ability to emerge from the dust and debris of battle seemingly unstoppable, executing coordinated strikes with eerie silence and devastating accuracy.
