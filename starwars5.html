Overview

Foxtrot Company is the elite strike element of the 212th Battalion. Composed of veteran clones and hand-selected operatives, Foxtrot specializes in rapid eliminations, stealth infiltration, and high-value target neutralization. They operate behind enemy lines, often under blackout conditions, where silence and precision are paramount.

Mission Set

  • Primary: Covert elimination of high-value targets including enemy commanders and artillery crews.
  • Secondary: CQC assaults and fast clearances of enemy strongholds.
  • Tertiary: Disruption of command networks and coordination of black ops within the 212th.

Training

Members of Foxtrot undergo advanced close-quarters combat and stealth training beyond standard clone protocols. Every operative is skilled in unarmed combat, infiltration, demolition, and silent takedowns. Tryouts are rigorous, testing reflexes, endurance, and mental fortitude.

Philosophy

Foxtrot stands apart — they are the blade in the dark. They believe in efficiency over spectacle, operating with discipline and discretion. Their motto: “Strike unseen, leave nothing standing.”

ELITE: Born from the fires of battle, Foxtrot are the unseen executioners of the 212th.

STRIKE: When others hesitate, they act — fast, quiet, final.

LEGACY: Their deeds are whispers in the archives, but their impact echoes across the stars.
